about

The Rays were originally named the Tampa Bay Devil Rays when they were founded in 1998, and changed their name to the Tampa Bay Rays in 2007. They were the 13th and 14th expansion teams in Major League baseball history. The Rays had an overall record of 1,912 wins and 2,034 losses since 1998, for a winning percentage of 48.5%. The Rays' mascot is Raymond. The Rays have appeared in eight postseasons and won two American League pennants, in 2008 and 2020.The Tampa Bay Rays are an American professional baseball team based in the Tampa Bay area. The Rays compete in Major League Baseball as a member club of the American League East division, and are one of two major league clubs based in Florida, alongside the National League's of Miami Marlins.
